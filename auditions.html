📍 Lagos, Nigeria 📅 Deadline: 30th March 2026 💰 Paid Roles 🎭 Ages 18 and above

Project Overview

On Lagos Hills follows the lives of three brothers and their younger sister trying to build meaningful lives in the restless and ever-moving city of Lagos.

After the sudden death of their father, the siblings are forced to rely on one another more than ever before. Living together in a modest apartment on the outskirts of the city, they struggle to balance family loyalty, personal ambition, relationships, and the pressure to succeed in a city that never slows down.

Each sibling carries a different dream.

  • The eldest brother feels the weight of responsibility on his shoulders, trying to hold the family together while sacrificing parts of his own ambition.
  • The second brother is driven and restless, determined to break into Lagos' competitive business world, even if it means making difficult choices.
  • The youngest brother is still discovering who he is, caught between expectations and his own passion for creativity.
  • Their sister, sharp and resilient, is determined to prove that she is more than the "little one" of the family as she fights to carve her own path in a society that often underestimates her.

Through moments of joy, heartbreak, laughter, and conflict, On Lagos Hills explores the realities of modern Nigerian youth — ambition, survival, loyalty, and the constant question of how far one is willing to go to achieve their dreams.

At its core, the story is about family, identity, and the courage to chase purpose in a city that challenges everyone who dares to dream.
