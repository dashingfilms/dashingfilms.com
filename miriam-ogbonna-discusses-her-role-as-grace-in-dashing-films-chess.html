Miriam Ogbonna Discusses Her Role as Grace in Dashing Films' "Chess"

📅 April 27, 2026 🕐 4 min read ✍️ Dashing Films

Dashing Films is set to release its upcoming web series Chess on Saturday, May 2nd, 2026, with a simultaneous YouTube premiere on the official Dashing Films channel and organised watch parties scheduled across Lagos, Anambra, and Imo State.

As part of the lead-up to the release, cast members have begun sharing insights into their characters and production experience. Actress Miriam Ogbonna, who plays the role of Grace, discusses her involvement in the series and the emotional depth behind her character.

About Grace: On the surface, she presents as warm, gentle, and emotionally approachable. Beneath that softness lies a more complex emotional structure — a tendency to dwell on negativity and overlook the positive aspects of situations.

Miriam describes Grace as a character shaped by contrast. On the surface, she presents as warm, gentle, and emotionally approachable. However, beneath that softness lies a more complex emotional structure defined by a tendency to dwell on negativity and overlook the positive aspects of situations. According to Miriam, this mindset plays a significant role in the direction of Grace's journey within the story, eventually leading to a personal loss that shifts her path.

She explains that what drew her to the role was the strength and emotional layering of the character. Grace was not written as a simple personality, but as a reflection of internal conflict and human imperfection, which made the role immediately compelling to her as an actor.

On Set Experience

Miriam notes that her experience on set was smooth and well-supported. She describes the production environment as accommodating, which allowed her to focus fully on performance and character delivery without distraction. This, she adds, contributed to how naturally she was able to connect with the emotional demands of the role.

The Message of Chess

Speaking on the broader message of Chess, Miriam highlights the series' focus on human misunderstanding and internal struggle. She explains that the story reflects individuals who are constantly battling unseen personal issues while navigating relationships and life decisions. Rather than portraying idealised characters, the series leans into emotional realism and flawed humanity.

As anticipation builds toward the release date, she hints that Grace is a character designed to unfold gradually. Her personality and motivations are revealed in layers, allowing viewers to connect with her journey over time rather than immediately.

Chess premieres Saturday, May 2nd, 2026 on the Dashing Films YouTube channel, with watch parties in Lagos, Anambra, and Imo State.
