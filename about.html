About Dashing Films

Who we are, what we believe, and why we create

Who We Are

Dashing Films is an independent film production company dedicated to telling authentic and emotionally compelling stories that connect with audiences across cultures. Driven by a passion for storytelling and visual creativity, the company produces films that explore human relationships, faith, ambition, and real-life experiences.

Committed to meaningful cinema, Dashing Films believes that film has the power to inspire, challenge perspectives, and spark important conversations. Through collaboration with talented filmmakers, actors, and creatives, the company continues to develop projects that reflect quality storytelling and artistic excellence.

With a growing presence in the African film industry, Dashing Films aims to create bold, relatable stories that resonate with audiences both locally and globally.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To develop and produce compelling films that reflect authentic human experiences, spark meaningful conversations, and connect deeply with audiences through powerful storytelling and cinematic excellence.

Our Vision

To build a dynamic film production platform that champions bold storytelling and contributes to the global recognition and evolution of African cinema.

Meet the Founder

Jennifer Okorie

Founder & Filmmaker

Jennifer Okorie is a filmmaker, producer, and storyteller driven by a deep desire to bring meaningful human experiences to the screen. As the founder of Dashing Films, she approaches filmmaking not simply as a business, but as a powerful medium for telling stories that matter.

Her journey into film was not motivated by the industry itself, but by a personal story she felt compelled to tell — a story rooted in real emotions, lived experiences, and the complexities of life that many people silently carry. That desire to give voice to untold and deeply human narratives became the foundation of her work as a filmmaker.

Through Dashing Films, Jennifer focuses on developing films that explore relationships, faith, ambition, and the emotional realities of everyday life. Her storytelling approach is intentional and reflective, aiming to create films that resonate with audiences on a deeper level and spark meaningful conversations.

Beyond filmmaking, Jennifer is also an entrepreneur and the founder of the Jennifer Okorie Foundation, where she extends her commitment to impact and community development. Her vision for Dashing Films is to build a platform where powerful stories can be told with honesty, creativity, and purpose — contributing to the growth of African cinema while connecting with audiences around the world.
